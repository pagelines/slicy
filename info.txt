<h3>What is Slicy Slider?</h3>

Slicy Slider is a jQuery slider with a nice sliding effect and a number of options including custom links, custom text fields and more. Slicy Slider is beautiful responsive and supports up to 30 slides of your choice.

Want a slice? <strong>Grab it while it lasts! :)</strong>
